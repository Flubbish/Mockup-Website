A Minecraft Movie (2025)

Prepare for an epic adventure as Minecraft, the best-selling video game of all time, comes to life in its first-ever live-action film. Directed by Jared Hess ("Nacho Libre") and starring Jason Momoa and Jack Black, this thrilling story follows four misfits who are pulled into the Overworld, a unique, cubic world where creativity and survival go hand in hand.

Faced with Piglins, Zombies, and the challenges of mastering this new realm, Garrett (Momoa) and his team must join forces with expert crafter Steve (Black) to protect the Overworld and find their way back home. Packed with excitement, imagination, and heart, “A Minecraft Movie” promises to inspire audiences of all ages.
